# Brand

## Name

**Joch** is German for **yoke**.

A yoke is the frame used to harness working animals together so they can pull with coordination, direction, and force.

For AI agents:

```text
Raw intelligence is power.
Joch gives it alignment.
```

## Brand promise

> **Joch AI harnesses autonomous agents into accountable workforces.**

Expanded:

```text
Joch helps teams know what agents exist,
what they can access,
which tools they can call,
what they did,
what they cost,
and whether they are safe to run.
```

## Brand belief

AI agents will not fail in production because they are not smart enough.

They will fail because nobody knows:

```text
what they are allowed to do
which tools they can touch
what data they can access
who owns them
what they cost
what they changed
why they acted
whether they can be trusted
```

Joch AI exists to make autonomous agents **visible, governed, portable, and accountable**.

## Brand pillars

| Pillar | Meaning | Message |
|---|---|---|
| Harness | control, direction, coordination, alignment | Put your agents under control without killing their usefulness. |
| Govern | policies, permissions, approvals, budgets, data boundaries | Agents can act, but only within the rules you define. |
| Observe | model calls, tool calls, memory writes, RAG retrievals, approvals, costs | Know what every agent did, used, changed, and cost. |
| Port | cross-framework, cross-model, cross-runtime, cross-cloud | Bring any agent. Run it anywhere. Govern it the same way. |
| Prove | ABOMs, traces, evals, release gates, audit logs, policy history | Do not trust an agent because it worked once. Trust it because it is measured. |

## Tone

Joch AI should sound:

```text
plainspoken
technical
disciplined
operator-minded
anti-hype
slightly rebellious
```

Avoid:

```text
magic
digital gods
AI coworkers
AGI hype
limitless autonomy
agentic transformation
```

Preferred language:

```text
harness
govern
audit
trace
own
deploy
route
approve
record
contain
operate
```
